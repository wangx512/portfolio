XIAOYU WANG

ABOUT

Xiaoyu Wang is a passionate and diligent designer who focuses on UI/UX and human-centered design. She is devoted to creating works that aim for a balance between aesthetics and practicality. As a person who likes to observe human behaviors and emotions, Xiaoyu enjoys the process of conducting user research thus developing a deeper understanding of users. Her goal as a designer is to entertain as well as serve users thus creating the best experience for them. She received her BFA in New Media Design from Rochester Institute of Technology in 2021 and is currently pursuing a Communication Design master's at Parsons School of Design. She will graduate in Spring 2022 and is looking for a full-time UI/UX position that can help her to become a more professional designer in the future.
